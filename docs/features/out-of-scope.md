# Out Of Scope

This document exists to protect the project from becoming too large.

## Not a CRM

Do not add:

- customers;
- leads;
- deals;
- follow-ups;
- customer history;
- loyalty logic.

## Not a warehouse

Do not add:

- stock balances;
- stock movements;
- write-offs;
- incoming shipments;
- suppliers;
- warehouse reports.

## Not a cash register

Do not add:

- fiscal receipts;
- shifts;
- payments;
- refunds;
- tax reporting;
- cashier sessions.

## Not analytics platform

Do not add:

- category analytics;
- predictions;
- dashboards with many charts;
- seller ranking;
- advanced BI.
